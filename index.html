About

Hi, there. I'm a freelance writer / graphic designer based out of Hollidaysburg, PA. I am a huge fan of Twix candy bars, Cocoa Pebbles cereal, golden retrievers, and the color blue. This is my portfolio and welcome to it.

How are you feeling today? I am well, thank you very much for asking. The weather's been tolerable lately. That helps keep things on the up and up.

I've been working with digital media for several years now. Video and radio production were where I started with this type of work. Then I gradually moved into web content writing, some graphic design, and some very basic web development work. Now, I provide serveral clients with ongoing graphic design support for their websites and social media channels. I'm also actively working on expanding my web development skills and capabilities.
