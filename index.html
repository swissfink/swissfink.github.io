About

Hi, there. I'm a freelance writer / graphic designer / web developer based out of Hollidaysburg, PA. I'm a big fan of Twix® candy bars, Fruity Pebbles™ Cereal, golden retrievers, websites, and classic, pastel colors. This is my portfolio and welcome to it.

With more than 10 years of experience in creating and publishing new media content, I have developed a strong ability to work both the technical (e.g. database backend) and creative (e.g. outlining and mockup) aspects of digital marketing platforms. I'm passionate about problem-solving using effective storytelling, and I work well in a team environment or independently.
